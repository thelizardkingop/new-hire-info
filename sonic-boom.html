Sonic Boom AVD Signup Checklist

  1. Insert your CAC into your computer.
  2. Open a browser and go to https://usaf.dps.mil/sites/SBVP/SitePages/AVD-Pilot-Signup.aspx
  3. Enter your .mil email and select the CAC option at the next pop-up.
  4. On the webpage that opens, scroll down and click the Sonic Boom: AVD Pilot Signup button.
  5. Click the Start now button that comes up on the next screen.
  6. Select the Yes or No radio buttons as appropriate and click Submit.
  7. A new page will load saying you've been added to the ILZ AVD Onboarding List. Onboarding may have been paused. If that is the case, you will need to pick up from page 4 of the guide once you receive an email from HQ CCC ILZ Cloud Support via NIPR.

The migration to the new VM was supposed to have been done before 01MAR25, so you will need to follow the guide slightly differently.
